Welcome to Console Rock Paper Scissors!
To play press Ctrl+Shift+J (Windows) or Cmd+Option+J (Mac) to open the console!
These are the rules to playing Rock Paper Scissors:
  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock
These are the different options to play Rock Paper Scissors
Type "Rock" to pick Rock
Type "Paper" to pick Paper
Type "Scissors" to pick Scissors
